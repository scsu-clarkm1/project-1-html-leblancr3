Ingredients Needed

Make sure you have the following on hand before you begin.

  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 cup Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Frozen Mango
  • 1 cup Almond Milk
  • 1 serving Vanilla Protein Powder (optional)

Instructions

Follow the below instructions carefully for a delicious smoothie. Take your time and you will be rewarded.

  1. Simply place all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend, Pour, and Enjoy!
